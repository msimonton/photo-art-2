About Me

I am a part-time photograhper, sports, travel and art lover. In late 2014, while persuing website Digital Photography School, by Darren Rowse of Australia, I cam across an online photoshop class offered by an amazing teacher, Sebastian Michaels (USA) entitled Photoshop Grunge. Subsequentely, Sebastion offered a year long advanced photoshop class called AWAKE, Living the Photoartistic Life, and a black and white course called Createve Black and WHite. Through these courses and learning several photo editing software programs, primarily Adobe Photoshop CC and Lightroom CC, I discovered a fabulous new world of creating my own "art" from photos.
I am a fan of modern art styles including impressionist paintings, every day street scenes and high resolution black and shites, and surreal works. I hope you enjoy the projects on this site, and that maybe you'll be inspired to try some of it yourself!

DPS is a wonderful resource for all things photography. I purchased some ebooks from DPS and was entered into a drawling... and I won! I got a $1000 Nikkon lens! Adobe Creative Cloud

Adobe, maker of Photoshop, Lightroom, Muse, Dreamweaver, In-Design, and many other amazing apps, is the king of photoediting software. The Creative Cloud membership gives you access to its programs, stock photos, cloud storage and many other useful resources. It cannot be done without

Topaz Labs

Topaz Labs makes plug-ins for Photoshop that allows you to turn photos into paintings and other works of art. Topaz Impression is my go-to filter for photoart projects.

Photoshop Artisty

Sebastian Michaels is an incredible Photoshop, Lightroom and other photo-editing program teacher, and artist coach. He offers several amazing online courses. He changed my world, and is the primary reason I wanted to become an artist, as well as a photographer.
